The Program


The 365x Scale-Up Program is a business enhancer for early- revenue Software and IoT Startup Companies. Alongside Fortune500 Coaches and Mentors, our startups scale-up their business, align with market needs, connect with VC's and investors for A and B fundraising rounds, and engage with targeted customers and distribution channels with the support of our Strategic Partners.
